About

An international trader, built for executable deals.

Ozofryn is an international commodity trading company engaged in the physical supply of crude oil, refined petroleum products, and selected fertilizers. Our business is grounded in commercial clarity, reliable counterparties, and practical execution.

Who we are

Focused on physical trade. Not paper.

We operate across supply-rich origins and demand-driven markets, engaging in transactions that are meaningful in both scale and intent. Every deal we pursue is structured around real supply, real demand, and terms that can perform.

Our presence across India and the UAE gives us commercial reach into South Asia, the MENA region, and the bunker markets of the Arabian Peninsula. Our partner network extends performance across Europe, Africa, and the Far East.

What guides us

Four principles that define how we trade.

01 Principle

Executable, not indicative

We only pursue deals that have real supply, real buyers, and can complete on agreed terms. No speculative chains, no unverifiable claims.

02 Principle

Transparent pricing

All pricing references recognized market benchmarks: Platts, Argus, or buyer-agreed formulas. No opaque discounting or parallel markets.

03 Principle

Compliance first

Counterparty, cargo, and documentation are checked against international sanctions and maritime standards before we proceed.

04 Principle

Long-term relationships

We would rather build a term programme with a credible counterparty than close ten one-off trades. Our model is repeat business.
