Refined Petroleum Products.

Refined petroleum products are the core of Ozofryn's trading activity. We operate across supply structures where pricing, product availability, and delivery feasibility support executable business.

01 Distillate

Diesel 10 ppm

Low-sulphur automotive diesel supplied to regional and export markets. FOB or CIF basis on request.

02 Distillate

AGO

Automotive Gas Oil for industrial and transport fuel markets where specification meets buyer requirements.

03 Distillate

ULSD

Ultra-Low-Sulphur Diesel for markets requiring the tightest distillate sulphur specifications.

04 Marine

MGO

Marine Gasoil supplied to bunker markets and vessels requiring a clean distillate for MARPOL-compliant operation.

05 Marine

VLSFO

Very-Low-Sulphur Fuel Oil (≤0.5%) for compliance with IMO 2020 marine-sulphur regulations.

06 Marine

HSFO

High-Sulphur Fuel Oil for vessels equipped with scrubbers and for markets accepting higher-sulphur grades.

Supply structures

Execution matters as much as price.

Specification, terminal position, logistics, documentation, and timing determine whether a transaction can move smoothly. Our focus is to structure refined product business that is practical, clear, and commercially workable.

  • Bulk cargo and storage-based transactions
  • Tank-to-tank supply and ISO tanks where suitable
  • Bunkering and marine fuel supply
  • Pricing linked to Platts / Argus benchmarks where applicable
  • Delivery basis: FOB, CIF, or DES on request
